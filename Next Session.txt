SESSION HANDOFF — 2026-05-09 (session 4)
=============================

WHAT WAS DONE THIS SESSION
-----------------------------
1. Fixed SA swap overlap bug — the root cause of building overlaps.
   SA was checking fits(a at b's pos) && fits(b at a's pos) independently,
   so different-sized buildings could end up at mutually overlapping positions.
   Fix: temporarily add na to occ after first check, then check nb.
   Console now shows [OVERLAP after SA] clean / 108 cells before fix.

2. Fixed resolveOverlaps off-site building relocation bug.
   Off-site buildings failed fitsAt at original position (cells not in unlocked),
   causing the scan to relocate them INTO the unlocked area as phantom buildings.
   Fix: added isOnsite check — off-site buildings skip the scan entirely.

3. Fixed resolveOverlaps !placed cascade bug.
   When an on-site building found no valid slot, it was pushed to resolved
   without marking occ, allowing later buildings to land at the same position.
   Fix: mark(b, b.x, b.y, occ) even on !placed.

4. Added detectOverlaps() helper — called after SA / resolveOverlaps / ensureRoadAccess.
   Logs [OVERLAP after X] clean or lists first 5 offending pairs.
   Leave in for now; remove before final ship.

5. Added "Roads to Victory" set (Statue of Honor + Road to Victory).
   Statue of Honor comes in Lv. 1-8 ("Name - Lv. N" suffix format).

6. Fixed leveled building name matching — three normalization patterns:
   - "Lv. N - Name"  (prefix)   → strip to "Name"
   - "Name - Lv. N"  (suffix)   → strip to "Name"
   - "Name (Era)"    (trailing)  → strip to "Name"
   Cherry Garden pieces (Nishikigoi Pond, Sakura Rock) now match their
   level variants. BUILDING_SETS pieces stay as base names.

7. Added set-based clustering in bScore (setW = 1.5).
   Buildings in the same BUILDING_SET now attract each other in SA,
   independent of building type (critical for Roads to Victory where
   Statue of Honor is "residential" and Road to Victory is "decoration").
